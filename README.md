## **Key Features**

### **Algebraic & Geometric Features**
**Quadratic Solver:** Finds the roots of quadratic equations. Features a user-selectable rounding precision(ex: set to 2 for hundredths, 1 for tenths).

**Slope Finder:** Calculates the slope between any two Cartesian points(($x_1,y_1$) & ($x_2,y_2$)).

**Coordinate Geometry:** Input 2 points to calculate the midpoint, distance, or the resulting linear function in slope-intercept form($y=mx+b$).

**Pythagorean Theorem:** Solves for the missing leg or hypotenuse of a right triangle.

**2D Area Finder:** Calculates the area of various common 2D shapes.

**3D Surface Area:** Solves for lateral and/or total surface area of common 3D geometric solids.

### **Advanced Trigonometric Features**

**Law of Sines:** Solves for missing angles and sides in non-right triangles.

**Law of Cosines:** Solves for Missing side or angle when given SAS or SSS.

**Heron's Formula:** Calculates the semi-perimeter, perimeter, and total area of any triangle when only the lengths of all three sides are known.

**Euler's Polyhedral Formula:** Test for convex/non-convex polyhedra using $V-E+F=2$ and $V-E+F=2-2g$.

### **Physics & Electronics**

**Newton's Laws:** Solves for Force($N$), Mass($kg$), and Acceleration($m/s^2$), Weight Force($N$), and Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation($N$).

**Ohm's Law:** Calculates basic electrical values including Voltage($V$), Current($I$), and Resistance($R$).

### **Utilities & Customization**

**Customizable Notes:** Comes pre-loaded with the Law of Exponents, Soh-Cah-Toa, and Cosine Laws. Users can easily edit this file to add custom notes/cheat sheets using TI Connect or Cemetech SourceCoder 3.

**System Clock:** Displays the current time and date directly from the calculator's internal clock(Note: Date and time must be set in the mode menu).


## **Install Directions**
1. Download both .8xp files from the repository.
2. Connect your calculator to your computer using its provided mini-USB cable.
3. For Windows/Mac, [install TI Connect CE](https://education.ti.com/en/software/details/en/CA9C74CAD02440A69FDC7189D7E1B6C2/swticonnectcesoftware) (if not already installed). For Linux, [install TILP](https://sourceforge.net/projects/tilp/) (if not already installed).
4. Drag and drop .8xp files from Downloads and send.
5. Press **prgm** button on your calculator to run the program(s)


## **Compatibility**
This program was written specifically for the eZ80 line of Texas Instruments calculators and uses functions/commands introduced in OS 5.2. It is fully compatible with:

- TI-84 Plus CE
- TI-84 Plus CE-T
- TI-83 Premium CE

*Note: Due to the use of newer OS 5.2 commands/functions, this is not guaranteed to work nor display correctly on older smaller monochrome calculators such as the standard TI-84/83 Plus.*
